Email Encryption

North Florida Thyroid Center · Capital Area Healthy Start

Microsoft 365 Azure Rights Management Microsoft Purview DLP HIPAA

Project goals & scope

Healthcare clients needed to email protected health information (PHI) to patients and providers without violating HIPAA. The goal was to guarantee that any message containing sensitive data — diagnoses, dates of birth, medical record numbers — is automatically encrypted in transit, while keeping the process simple enough for non-technical office staff.

Scope covered the full encryption pipeline in Microsoft 365: activating Azure Rights Management, building Data Loss Prevention (DLP) policies with custom sensitive-information types, creating mail-flow (transport) rules to trigger encryption, and producing the supporting documentation the practice needs for compliance.

My specific role

As the consultant and sole implementer, I:

  • Activated and configured Azure Rights Management in each tenant.
  • Authored DLP compliance policies with custom sensitive-information types, including a UTF-16 BOM-encoded XML rule package to detect dates of birth.
  • Built bracketed-keyword transport rules so staff can force encryption by adding [secure] to a subject line.
  • Created recipient-facing email templates, a PHI Disclosure Log workbook, and CCD medical-records transmittal templates.
  • Tested end-to-end delivery and trained staff on day-to-day use.

Phishing & Spam Protection

Twisted Pair Technology & healthcare clients

SPF DKIM DMARC Microsoft Defender

Project goals & scope

Email is the number-one entry point for attacks against small medical and professional offices. The goal was to cut phishing and spoofing by verifying that inbound mail is genuinely from who it claims to be, and by filtering malicious messages before they reach users.

Scope covered the email-authentication stack and filtering layer across multiple domains: publishing and tuning SPF records, enabling DKIM signing, deploying DMARC with a phased enforcement policy, and configuring Microsoft Defender anti-spam and anti-phishing protection — including domains hosted on Microsoft DNS and on cPanel.

My specific role

As the consultant and sole implementer, I:

  • Configured full SPF, DKIM, and DMARC for twisttechnology.com (Microsoft-hosted DNS), with DKIM enabled and DMARC set to p=quarantine; pct=25 for a controlled, phased rollout.
  • Began the same authentication build-out for thyroidcenter.com, where DNS is managed in cPanel.
  • Tuned Microsoft Defender anti-spam and anti-phishing policies to quarantine spoofed and malicious mail.
  • Monitored DMARC aggregate reports to confirm legitimate mail passed before raising enforcement.
  • Documented the configuration so the policy can be advanced from monitoring to full enforcement safely.
