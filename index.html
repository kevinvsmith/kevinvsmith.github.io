About Me

IT Consultant &
Business Owner

I secure the systems and data that organizations can't afford to lose. Over 20+ years working with healthcare, accounting, and legal practices, I've operated on a simple principle: organizations with the most to protect often have the least security support, and that's exactly where I do my best work.

My focus is on the security fundamentals that actually prevent breaches. I've built HIPAA-compliant email encryption with custom data-loss prevention policies, designed phishing defense programs for medical offices, hardened networks against intrusion, and created sensitive-data controls that keep regulated organizations compliant and protected. I approach every environment the same way an attacker would, looking for the gap before someone else finds it.

What sets my security work apart is that it holds up in the real world. A policy nobody follows protects nothing, so I build defenses people understand and adopt, and I document them so they survive past my engagement. That blend of technical rigor and clear communication is why clients in regulated industries trust me with their most sensitive systems.

I'm deepening that foundation with a B.S. in Information Technology at FSU, concentrating in cybersecurity, pairing two decades of hands-on defense with current, formal security training.
